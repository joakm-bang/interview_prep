# Data intuition and debugging: Practice problems

1. A key conversion metric rises 18% overnight with no known launch. Lead the investigation.
2. Aggregate experiment lift is positive, iOS positive, Android negative, web flat. Separate heterogeneity, Simpson's paradox, instrumentation, and multiplicity.
3. Revenue rises 12%, orders fall 8%, active users are flat. Decompose and explain.
4. Two dashboards reporting the same metric differ by 7%. Reconcile them.
5. A survey has 6% response and treatment changes response probability. Explain selection bias and sensitivity analyses.
6. Offline NDCG improves while purchases decline. Develop hypotheses and an online test.
7. A voluntarily adopted feature appears successful. Explain why this is not causal and propose designs.
8. An obscure segment suddenly becomes 25% of users. List checks before interpretation.
