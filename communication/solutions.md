# Communication drills: Solutions

A strong answer begins with the conclusion and structure, not a chronological data dump.

Example opener:

> I would separate this into measurement validity, population changes, and genuine behavior. First I would verify the metric and logging; second inspect mix shifts; third test product hypotheses.

For project stories use: problem, stakes, your role, decision, technical approach, cross-functional influence, result, and lesson.

For prioritization, score expected impact, urgency, confidence, strategic alignment, and effort. State explicitly what will not be done and why.
